Software analysis and design

1. Origin of the project

Regarding the origin of the idea, it can come from:

  • The compamy itself, as one of its internal developments.
  • An external entity/company which asks this company to develop the product.

In both cases, we must have a clear overview of the product to be developed, and evaluate its viability.

The project manager

The project manager is an experienced person who will be in charge of coordinated the development process in general. A factor would be:

- The chosen candidate should have a proven experience in the business area of the project, besides having good interpersonal relationships and group management.

The project manager must do tasks like:

  • Set budgets, calendars and tasks assignements
  • Support and provide communication techniques among the team members.

3. Gathering requirements

The first stage in the software development process is the requirements analysis, which is divided into 2 subtasks: requirements specifications and software analysis.
